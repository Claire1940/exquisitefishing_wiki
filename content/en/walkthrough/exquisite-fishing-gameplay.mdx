The **exquisite fishing gameplay** experience is built upon a precision-based pixel art simulation where players navigate a boat across various ocean biomes to collect rare marine life. Unlike traditional fishing games that rely purely on RNG, this title requires active participation in guiding the lure to specific depths while managing the "Hook Meter." Success depends on your ability to match the correct equipment with the environmental conditions of the ocean you are currently exploring.

To maximize your efficiency, you must understand the three phases of every catch: casting, attraction, and reeling. When you cast your line, you can influence the horizontal movement of the lure as it sinks. This is vital for avoiding common fish that might deplete your line's durability or waste time. Once the lure reaches a target creature, an attraction phase begins where you must keep the target engaged until the Hook Meter is completely filled.

| Phase | Action | Primary Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Casting** | Manual Lure Guidance | Reach target depth/avoid trash fish |
| **Attraction** | Maintain Proximity | Fill the Hook Meter to secure catch |
| **Reeling** | Surface Recovery | Bring the secured catch to the boat |
| **Selling** | Market Exchange | Convert catches into gold for upgrades |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Strategy">
If you are targeting a specific creature like the Mermaid or Shark, ignore all other fish silhouettes. Wasting a cast on a low-value fish reduces your gold-per-hour efficiency significantly.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Ocean Biomes and Progression Roadmap">

Progression in the game is strictly linear, defined by the depth of the water and the quality of your vessel. There are four primary ocean biomes, each demanding a higher entry fee or a more durable boat. As you move from the Calm Ocean to the Dark Ocean, the environmental hazards increase, but the financial rewards for successful catches grow exponentially.

<Callout type="warning" title="Budget Management">
Never spend all your gold on gifts. Always ensure you have enough remaining to afford the entry cost of the next biome or the boat upgrade required to reach it.
</Callout>
