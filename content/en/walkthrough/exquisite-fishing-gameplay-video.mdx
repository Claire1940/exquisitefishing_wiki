<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Beginner Progression Route">

To progress efficiently without getting stuck on expensive late-game upgrades, follow this optimized step-by-step path from the starting Calm Ocean to the deepest parts of the Dark Ocean.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Farm the Calm Ocean Mermaid">
    Start in the Calm Ocean using the free Regular Old Boat. Purchase the **Diamond Ring** and equip your **Regular Hook** to catch the Mermaid at 50m for an easy 300g.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Target the Calm Ocean Shark">
    Reinvest your early gold to purchase the **Good Hook** and **Striped Pantie**. Lower your line to 200m to catch the Shark, earning 2,000g per catch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock the Calm Ocean Angler">
    Purchase the **Box of Chocolates** and equip the **Regular Hook**. Cast to 300m to catch the Angler. This is your primary early-game gold farming method, yielding 10,000g per successful catch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transition to the Rocky Ocean">
    Spend 1,500g on the **Regular Boat** to unlock the Rocky Ocean. Focus on purchasing the **Reinforced Hook** and **Vibrant Rose** to catch the 600m Angler for 13,000g.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Retrieve Grandpa's Letter in the Turbulent Ocean">
    Purchase the **Reinforced Boat** for 5,000g. Farm the 900m Shark (using the **Super Hook** and **Diamond Ring**) for 24,000g, and make sure to descend to 1,000m to collect **Grandpa's Letter**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Conquer the Dark Ocean Depths">
    Buy the **Golden Boat** for 8,000g. Gradually upgrade your hooks in depth order. Your final goal is the 2,000m Angler in the Dark Ocean, which requires the **Omega Hook** and **Diamond Ring** and pays 45,000g.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Milestone Unlocked">
Completing Step 6 and catching the 2,000m Dark Ocean Angler completes the deepest documented fishing objective in the game.
</Callout>
