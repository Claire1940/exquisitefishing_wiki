<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Biome Progression">

Navigating the ocean depths requires a strict upgrade path. Follow this step-by-step sequence to optimize your gold earnings and unlock every ocean biome without getting stuck.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Dominate the Calm Ocean">
    Start with the free Regular Old Boat. Cast your line and guide the lure toward the 50m mark to catch the Mermaid using a **Regular Hook** and **Diamond Ring** for 300g. Once you save up, upgrade your setup to catch the 200m Shark, and finally farm the 300m Angler for a massive 10,000g payout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Conquer the Rocky Ocean">
    Purchase the Regular Boat for 1,500g to unlock the Rocky Ocean. Here, targets range from 100m down to 600m. Focus on catching the Kappa at 500m (7,500g) and the Angler at 600m (13,000g) using the **Reinforced Hook** and **Vibrant Rose**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Survive the Turbulent Ocean">
    Spend 5,000g on the Reinforced Boat. This biome introduces the Whale at 50m (2,500g) and the deep-sea Shark at 900m (24,000g). You must also reach the absolute bottom at 1,000m to retrieve **Grandpa's Letter**, a vital story collectible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach the Dark Ocean Abyss">
    Purchase the Golden Boat for 8,000g to access the final biome. This zone spans from 50m down to 2,000m. Upgrade your gear sequentially to handle the extreme tension, culminating in the 2,000m Angler catch using the **Omega Hook** and **Diamond Ring** for 45,000g.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Tension Warning">
Deeper fish pull back much harder. If you attempt to reel in a 1,500m+ target with an unupgraded hook, the line will snap instantly, losing both the catch and your equipped gift.
</Callout>

</Section>
