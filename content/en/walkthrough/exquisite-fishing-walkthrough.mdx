<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Gear & Gift Upgrade Priorities">

Your equipment dictates how deep your line can go and which creatures will interact with your lure. Boats open up new areas, hooks support the physical strain of deep catches, and gifts attract specialized targets.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Basic Gifts">
    Purchase the Diamond Ring and Striped Pantie first. These items allow you to catch the Mermaid and Shark in the starting Calm Ocean.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade to Good & Double Shielded Hooks">
    Buy these hooks to handle the tension of deeper Rocky Ocean creatures. The Double Shielded Hook is essential for catching the Octopus.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invest in the Reinforced Hook">
    This is your most versatile mid-game hook. It is used to catch the Rocky Ocean Angler and the Turbulent Ocean Mermaid.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Max Out with the Omega Hook">
    The ultimate endgame tool. Paired with the Diamond Ring, it is the only hook capable of reeling in the Dark Ocean Angler at 2,000m.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Below is a detailed breakdown of the critical gear pieces and their documented uses:

| Equipment Category | Item Name | Progression Stage | Documented Use | Known Target |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Boat | Regular Boat | Early game | Accesses Rocky Ocean | Octopus, Kappa |
| Boat | Reinforced Boat | Mid game | Accesses Turbulent Ocean | Whale, Shark |
| Boat | Golden Boat | Late game | Accesses Dark Ocean | Deep-sea monsters |
| Hook | Double Shielded Hook | Early-mid | Handles mid-depth tension | Octopus, Whale |
| Hook | Drill Hook | Mid game | Required for rocky bottoms | Kappa, Shark |
| Hook | Anal Beads Hook | Late game | Required for deep tentacles | Octopus |
| Hook | Omega Hook | Endgame | Reaches 2,000m depth | Angler |

<Callout type="info" title="Hook Mechanics">
Each hook has a specific tension rating. If you attempt to catch a deep-sea creature with an inferior hook, the line will snap, and you will lose both the catch and your active bait.
</Callout>
