To succeed in **exquisite fishing version 1.07**, you must master the fundamental loop of casting, attracting, and reeling. The game begins in the Calm Ocean, where you are provided with a Regular Old Boat for free. Your primary goal is to accumulate enough gold to purchase specialized hooks and gifts that attract "Special Creatures," which yield significantly higher rewards than standard fish.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="r1aja9_vSdg" title="Exquisite Fishing Gameplay and Mechanics" />

The fishing process involves throwing your lure and guiding it vertically. As you descend, you must avoid "trash" catches and position your lure near the silhouette of your intended target. Once a creature shows interest, a hook meter begins to fill. Keeping the target engaged until the meter is full secures the catch, after which you must reel it back to the surface.

| Action | Result | Strategy Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cast** | Lure enters water | Guide toward specific depth markers (50m, 200m, etc.) |
| **Attract** | Target approaches lure | Ensure correct **Gift** is equipped for that creature |
| **Hook** | Meter fills up | Maintain proximity to the target's center |
| **Sell** | Gold added to inventory | Visit the shop immediately to check for upgrades |

<Callout type="success" title="Starter Strategy">
In the very early game, target the **Mermaid** at 50m in the Calm Ocean. She requires a **Diamond Ring** and a **Regular Hook**. This catch provides a steady 300g, allowing you to quickly afford the **Good Hook** needed for the Shark.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Gear Upgrades and Loadout Priorities">

The shop in **exquisite fishing version 1.07** offers three main categories of items: Boats, Hooks, and Gifts. While Boats grant access to new areas, Hooks determine which "weight" of creature you can pull, and Gifts act as the bait for specific special characters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Hook Upgrades">
    Start by purchasing the **Good Hook**. This is necessary for the Calm Ocean Shark at 200m. The Shark is your first "big" earner at 2,000g per catch, which funds your transition to the Rocky Ocean.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Reinforced Hook Meta">
    Once in the Rocky Ocean, the **Reinforced Hook** becomes your workhorse. It is required for the Anemone and the deeper Angler. It offers the best balance of speed and durability for mid-game farming.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Specialized Shielded Hooks">
    As you enter the Turbulent Ocean, you will encounter creatures that put massive strain on your line. The **Double Shielded** and **Triple Shielded Hooks** are mandatory for the Whale and the 500m Kappa to prevent line snaps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Endgame Omega Gear">
    The final goal is the **Omega Hook**. This is the only gear capable of reeling in the 2,000m Angler in the Dark Ocean. Pair this with a **Diamond Ring** for the ultimate payout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Line Tension">
Using a hook that is "under-leveled" for a specific depth or creature weight will cause the tension meter to rise rapidly. If the meter maxes out, you lose the catch and the gift used. Always check the creature table before attempting a deep-sea catch.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Efficiency and Gold Farming Strategy">

To reach the endgame of **exquisite fishing version 1.07** efficiently, you should avoid "grinding" common fish. Instead, focus on a "Target Sprint" strategy. This involves identifying the highest-value creature you can currently catch and farming it until you can afford the next major progression item.

**The Optimal Farming Route:**
1.  **Stage 1**: Farm the **Calm Ocean Mermaid** (300g) until you can afford the **Striped Pantie** and **Good Hook**.
2.  **Stage 2**: Move to the **Calm Ocean Shark** (2,000g). This is the best gold-per-minute target in the early game.
3.  **Stage 3**: Save 1,500g for the **Regular Boat** to unlock the Rocky Ocean.
4.  **Stage 4**: Target the **Rocky Ocean Angler** at 600m (13,000g). This requires the **Reinforced Hook** and **Vibrant Rose**.
5.  **Stage 5**: Use the Angler profits to buy the **Reinforced Boat** (5,000g) and then the **Super Hook**.
6.  **Stage 6**: Farm the **Turbulent Ocean Shark** at 900m (24,000g) to fund the **Golden Boat** and **Omega Hook**.

| **Shark (Turbulent)** | Turbulent | 24,000g | High | High (Mid) |
| **Angler (Dark)** | Dark | 45,000g | Very High | Maximum |
