<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering Exquisite Fishing Controls and Reeling Mechanics">

To progress through the game's increasingly challenging biomes, players must master the **exquisite fishing controls**. The gameplay loop is straightforward but demands precision. You begin each run by selecting your boat and ocean biome. Once on the water, you cast your line and guide the descending lure. 

Guiding the hook requires careful maneuvering to bypass common fish and reach specific depth milestones where rare creatures reside. When a target bites, a hook meter appears on your screen. You must maintain steady tension—balancing your reel speed—to fill the meter completely. If you pull too fast or let the line slacken, the creature will escape.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tension Tip">
Never hold the reel button continuously. Tap the control rhythmically to keep the tension meter in the safe zone, especially when reeling in heavy deep-sea targets.
</Callout>

### Core Reeling & Controls Overview

| Mechanic | Control Input | Gameplay Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Casting** | Mouse Click / Button Press | Launches the lure into the active ocean biome |
| **Guiding** | Movement Keys / Left-Right | Steers the hook during descent to avoid unwanted targets |
| **Tension Control** | Tap/Release Reel Button | Balances the hook meter to secure active catches |
| **Selling** | Shop Interaction | Exchanges successfully reeled catches for gold |

</Section>

Attempting to fish in deep waters without the correct setup is a waste of resources. Below is the official progression roadmap for all four ocean biomes.

Using the wrong combination means the target will ignore your hook entirely. Plan your loadout carefully before leaving the dock.
