<Section icon={ShoppingCart} title="Essential Gear and Upgrade Priorities">

Your equipment determines which creatures you can attract and catch. Gear is divided into **Hooks**, **Gifts**, and **Boats**. While boats provide access to new areas, hooks and gifts work in tandem to trigger special encounters and scenes.

**Hook Categories:**
- **Standard Hooks**: Regular, Good, and Reinforced hooks for early to mid-game targets.
- **Shielded Hooks**: Double and Triple Shielded hooks to prevent losing heavy catches like the Whale.
- **Specialized Hooks**: Drill, Anal Beads, and Hearty hooks for specific creature requirements.
- **Endgame Hooks**: The Omega Hook is required for the deepest catch in the game.

<Callout type="warning" title="Loadout Warning">
Special creatures like the Mermaid or Shark will ignore your lure if you do not have their preferred gift equipped. Always check your **Fishipedia** for requirement hints.
</Callout>

**Gift Requirements for Special Catches:**
- **Diamond Ring**: Essential for the Mermaid (Calm) and Shark (Turbulent).
- **Striped Pantie**: Required for the Shark (Calm) and Whale (Turbulent).
- **Box of Chocolates**: Used for the Angler (Calm) and Anemone (Rocky).
- **Mighty Dildo**: Necessary for all Octopus encounters.
- **Vibrant Rose**: Unlocks deeper Angler and Kappa scenes.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Early Gold Farming Route">

To progress quickly through the **Exquisite Fishing beginner guide**, you need a reliable source of income. Following this specific route will allow you to unlock the Rocky Ocean and purchase mid-tier gear within your first hour of gameplay.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Mermaid Grind">
    Start in the Calm Ocean with the **Regular Hook** and **Diamond Ring**. Target the Mermaid at **50m**. She is worth 300g and is very easy to catch. Repeat this 5 times to fund your next gift.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Shark Upgrade">
    Purchase the **Good Hook** and **Striped Pantie**. Drop your lure to **200m** in the Calm Ocean. The Shark rewards you with **2,000g** per catch, significantly boosting your bankroll.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mastering the Angler">
    Equip the **Regular Hook** and **Box of Chocolates**. Dive to **300m** in the Calm Ocean. The Angler is the most valuable early target, paying out **10,000g**. Farm this until you have enough for the Regular Boat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transition to Rocky Ocean">
    Purchase the **Regular Boat** for 1,500g. Move to the Rocky Ocean and equip the **Double Shielded Hook** and **Mighty Dildo** to catch the Octopus at 100m for 1,500g.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
The Angler in the Calm Ocean remains one of the best gold-per-minute targets until you reach the Turbulent Ocean. If you find the deeper oceans too difficult, return to the Calm Ocean to farm the Angler.
</Callout>

</Section>
