<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the Fishing Technique">

The mechanical skill required for **exquisite fishing** is often underestimated. Many players simply click and hope for the best, but there is a specific rhythm to the "shot." Whether you are using a traditional rod or a specialized projectile-based fishing tool, the interaction with the water ripple is the determining factor for success.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Ripple">
    Wait for a circular ripple to appear on the water surface. Do not act immediately; wait for the ring to begin contracting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Tightening Phase">
    As the ripple gets tighter, it enters the "prime window." This is when the fish is most engaged with the surface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Precision Shot">
    Aim your tool at or slightly **below** the center of the ripple. Shooting above the ripple almost always results in a missed opportunity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Harvest">
    Once the hit registers, immediately retrieve the fish. In high-density spots, a new ripple may appear within seconds of your successful catch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The most common mistake is "late-shooting." If you wait until the ripple completely disappears, the fish has already retreated into the deep. Conversely, shooting too early when the ripple is wide will startle the fish, causing it to despawn. Mastery of the mid-contraction shot is essential for anyone serious about their **exquisite fishing guide** progression.

<Callout type="info" title="Aiming Mechanics">
The game's physics engine calculates the projectile's travel time and water resistance. Aiming slightly below the visual center of the ripple compensates for the refraction of light in the water, a common feature in 2026 gaming engines.
</Callout>

</Section>
