<Section icon={Swords} title="Technical Mastery: The Ripple Method">

Catching an exquisite isn't just about finding it; you must execute the catch perfectly. The **exquisite fishing discord** emphasizes the "Ripple Method." Unlike standard fishing where you might wait for a prompt, exquisite hunting requires active aiming and timing based on the visual feedback of the water's surface.

1.  **Locate the Ripple**: Look for the circular disturbances. Exquisites produce ripples that are more frequent and tighter than common fish.
2.  **Observe the Tightening**: Wait for the ripple to reach its smallest diameter. This is the "strike zone" window.
3.  **Aim Low**: Always shoot at or slightly **below** the ripple. Shooting above the ripple usually results in a missed opportunity.
4.  **Timing**: If you are late, the fish will submerged and despawn. You must be proactive once the ripple tightens.

| Action | Result | Community Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Shoot Above Ripple | **Miss** | Always aim for the center or lower |
| Shoot Tight Ripple | **Success** | Higher chance for Exquisite rarity |
| Shoot Wide Ripple | **Common Catch** | Usually results in Purple or Green tier |
| Late Shot | **Despawn** | Practice your draw speed in the Discord training spots |

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Mechanic">
The depth of the water doesn't just affect rarity; it also affects the ripple speed. In deeper water, ripples often tighten faster, requiring quicker reflexes.
</Callout>
