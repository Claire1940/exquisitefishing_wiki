The fishing loop begins with casting your lure and guiding it toward a target. Once a creature shows interest, you must fill the hook meter by keeping the target engaged before reeling the catch to the surface. Every successful catch is sold automatically, contributing to your gold reserves. In the early game, your primary goal is to move past the Regular Old Boat as quickly as possible to access the more lucrative Rocky Ocean.

<Callout type="tip" title="Initial Casting Tip">
When starting in the Calm Ocean, focus on the Mermaid at 50m. She only requires a Regular Hook and a Diamond Ring, providing a reliable 300g profit per catch to fund your first major equipment purchases.

Hooks are the most critical technical upgrade. Each hook has specific properties that allow it to catch certain creatures. For example, the **Double Shielded Hook** is a versatile mid-game tool used for both the Rocky Ocean Octopus and the Turbulent Ocean Whale.

| Hook Name | Known Targets | Best Use Case |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Wealth Generation Route">

To reach the endgame efficiently, follow this optimized gold-farming route. This sequence minimizes time spent on low-value fish and maximizes your gold-per-hour.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Calm Ocean Kickstart">
    Start by catching the Mermaid at 50m to earn your first few hundred gold. Once you have enough, purchase the **Good Hook** and **Striped Pantie** to target the Shark at 200m. This provides 2,000g per catch, which is enough to buy the Regular Boat (1,500g).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Angler Strategy">
    Before leaving the Calm Ocean, use the **Box of Chocolates** with your Regular Hook to catch the 300m Angler. Repeat this until you have roughly 10,000g. This surplus will fund your entry fees and equipment for the Rocky Ocean.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Rocky Ocean Scaling">
    Enter the Rocky Ocean and prioritize the **Kappa** at 500m (Drill Hook + Pearl Necklace) and the **Angler** at 600m (Reinforced Hook + Vibrant Rose). These catches pay 7,500g and 13,000g respectively, allowing you to save 5,000g for the Reinforced Boat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Endgame Deep Sea Push">
    In the Turbulent Ocean, focus on the 900m Shark (24,000g). Once you reach the Dark Ocean with the Golden Boat, your final goal is the 2,000m Angler. Use the **Omega Hook** and **Diamond Ring** to secure this 45,000g prize.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Saving for Boats">
Always keep a buffer of at least 2,000g when moving to a new ocean. If you spend all your gold on a boat and then fail your first few casts, you might find yourself stuck without enough gold to pay the entry fee for another trip.
</Callout>

</Section>
