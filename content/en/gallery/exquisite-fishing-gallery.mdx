### Core Fishing Mechanics
When you cast your line, you control the lateral movement of the lure as it sinks. To attract a special creature, your lure must carry the exact hook and gift combination required for that target. Once a creature bites, keep it engaged until the hook meter fills completely, then reel the catch back to the surface.
