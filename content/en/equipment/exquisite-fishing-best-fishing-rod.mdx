To afford the expensive late-game hooks and boats, you must follow an optimized gold farming path. This minimizes downtime and prevents you from getting stuck with low-yield catches.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Calm Ocean Mermaid Farm">
    Begin in the Calm Ocean using the free Regular Old Boat. Equip the **Regular Hook** and the **Diamond Ring** to target the Mermaid at 50m. Each catch yields **300g**, allowing you to build your starting fund.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Calm Ocean Shark Upgrade">
    Purchase the **Good Hook** and **Striped Pantie**. Lower your line to 200m in the Calm Ocean to catch the Shark. This upgrade increases your yield to **2,000g** per catch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Calm Ocean Angler Farming">
    Purchase the **Box of Chocolates** and re-equip your **Regular Hook**. Target the Angler at 300m. This is the best early-game farm, rewarding you with **10,000g** per catch. Use this to fund your entry to the Rocky Ocean.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Rocky Ocean Expansion">
    Spend 1,500g on the **Regular Boat** to enter the Rocky Ocean. Focus on purchasing the **Reinforced Hook** and **Vibrant Rose** to catch the 600m Angler for **13,000g** per run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Turbulent Ocean and Golden Boat Setup">
    Buy the **Reinforced Boat** (5,000g) and target the 900m Shark using the **Super Hook** and **Diamond Ring** for **24,000g**. Once you have accumulated enough funds, buy the **Golden Boat** (8,000g) to access the Dark Ocean.
  </Step>
</Steps>
