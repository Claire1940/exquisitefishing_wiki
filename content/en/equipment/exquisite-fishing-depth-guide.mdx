<Section icon={Shield} title="Gear Upgrades: Hooks and Boats">

Your equipment determines how deep you can go and what you can catch. Boats are the most expensive purchases, but they are essential for accessing new biomes. Hooks, on the other hand, are specialized tools. Some hooks provide better "shielding" against line tension, while others like the **Drill Hook** or **Omega Hook** are required for specific legendary creatures.

<Callout type="success" title="Equipment Strategy">
The **Reinforced Hook** is one of the most versatile mid-game tools. It is used for Anemones, Anglers, and Mermaids across multiple oceans, making it a high-priority purchase.
</Callout>

| Hook Tier | Primary Use | Documented Targets |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Regular Hook** | Early game farming | Mermaid (50m), Angler (300m) |
| **Good Hook** | Depth transition | Shark (200m) |
| **Double Shielded** | Tension management | Octopus (100m), Whale (50m) |
| **Drill Hook** | Deep rock penetration | Kappa (500m), Shark (500m) |
| **Anal Beads Hook** | Late game specialized | Octopus (700m, 1,500m) |
| **Omega Hook** | Final endgame tool | Angler (2,000m) |

When upgrading your boat, remember that each new vessel increases your maximum possible depth. The **Golden Boat** is required to survive the 2,000m descent in the Dark Ocean. Without it, your line will snap long before you reach the final Angler.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimized Gold Farming Route">

To complete the game efficiently, you should follow a specific spending route. Many players get stuck in the mid-game because they buy every gift in the shop, leaving them with no gold for the next boat. Follow these steps to maximize your earnings.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Calm Ocean Foundation">
    Start by catching the **Mermaid** at 50m using the Diamond Ring. Once you have enough, switch to the Box of Chocolates and farm the **300m Angler**. This creature provides 10,000g per catch, which is the best early-game return on investment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Rocky Ocean Expansion">
    Purchase the **Regular Boat** for 1,500g. Your new goal is the **600m Angler** (13,000g) and the **Kappa** (7,500g). Use the Drill Hook for the Kappa to ensure a successful catch. Save at least 5,000g for the next boat upgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Turbulent Ocean & The Letter">
    Buy the **Reinforced Boat**. Target the **900m Shark** for a massive 24,000g payout. While here, you must reach 1,000m to retrieve **Grandpa's Letter**, which is a critical story item. This biome is where gold starts to accumulate rapidly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dark Ocean Endgame">
    Purchase the **Golden Boat** (8,000g). This is the final stretch. Buy the **Omega Hook** and return to the Diamond Ring gift. Descend to 2,000m to catch the ultimate Angler, worth 45,000g. This catch marks the completion of the main progression route.
  </Step>
</Steps>
