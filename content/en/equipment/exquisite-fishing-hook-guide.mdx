<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Hook Upgrade Strategy">

To progress efficiently without wasting gold on unnecessary items, follow this optimized upgrade path. This strategy ensures you always have the right gear for the highest-paying target available at your current level.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Calm Ocean Foundation">
    Start by catching standard fish to buy the **Diamond Ring** and **Regular Hook**. Target the Mermaid at 50m for a quick 300g. Once you have a small buffer, buy the **Box of Chocolates** to catch the 300m Angler. This is your primary gold source for the early game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Rocky Ocean Transition">
    Save 1,500g for the **Regular Boat**. Upon entering the Rocky Ocean, prioritize the **Reinforced Hook** and **Vibrant Rose**. This combination allows you to catch the 600m Angler for 13,000g, which is significantly more efficient than farming the Calm Ocean.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Turbulent Depths and Story Items">
    Purchase the **Reinforced Boat** for 5,000g. Your goal here is twofold: reach 1,000m to find **Grandpa's Letter** and farm the 900m Shark (24,000g) using the **Super Hook** and **Diamond Ring**. This will fund your final boat upgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dark Ocean Mastery">
    Buy the **Golden Boat** (8,000g). Focus all your remaining gold on the **Omega Hook**. You will need to reuse gifts like the **Diamond Ring** and **Lovely Ribbon** to catch the endgame Whale (40,000g) and the ultimate Angler (45,000g) at the 2,000m floor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Hook Durability">
Higher-tier hooks like the **Triple Shielded Hook** or **Omega Hook** are not just for depth; they also make the reeling mini-game significantly easier by providing more "shielding" against target resistance. If you are struggling to reel in a catch, consider upgrading your hook even if you meet the minimum requirement.
</Callout>

</Section>
