<Section icon={Shield} title="Equipment Synergy: Hooks and Gifts">

Upgrading your boat is only half the battle. To successfully utilize **exquisite fishing boat upgrades**, you must synchronize your vessel with the appropriate **Hooks** and **Gifts**.
