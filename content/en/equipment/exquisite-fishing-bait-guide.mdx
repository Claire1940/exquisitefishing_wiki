<Section icon={Map} title="Calm Ocean: Early Game Bait Strategies">

The Calm Ocean is your starting biome and serves as the training ground for the **exquisite fishing bait guide**. <Section icon={Swords} title="Rocky Ocean: Mid-Game Progression Steps">

Once you have saved 1,500g, you can purchase the **Regular Boat** and access the Rocky Ocean. This biome introduces more complex requirements in the **exquisite fishing bait guide**. You will encounter the Octopus and the Kappa, both of which require specialized hooks like the **Double Shielded Hook** and the **Drill Hook**.

The Rocky Ocean is where equipment management becomes critical. You aren't just buying gifts anymore; you are managing hook durability and specific attraction rates. The Angler returns here at 600m, but it now requires a **Reinforced Hook** and a **Vibrant Rose**, significantly increasing the entry cost for that specific catch.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Turbulent and Dark Ocean: Endgame Loadouts">

The final stages of the **exquisite fishing bait guide** involve the Turbulent Ocean (5,000g entry) and the Dark Ocean (8,000g entry). The Dark Ocean is the ultimate test of your guiding skills. You must drop your lure down to 2,000m while avoiding dozens of obstacles.
