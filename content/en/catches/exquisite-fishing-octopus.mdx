<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Octopus Catching Guide">

Reaching the deep ocean floor and hauling back a massive Octopus takes preparation. Follow this optimized progression route to secure your first successful catch in the Rocky Ocean.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Purchase the Regular Boat Upgrade">
    Start in the Calm Ocean to farm basic catches and the early-game Angler. Accumulate **1,500g** to purchase the **Regular Boat**, which grants you entry to the **Rocky Ocean** biome.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip the Double Shielded Hook">
    Visit the shop and purchase the **Double Shielded Hook**. This specialized tackle is sturdy enough to withstand the pressure of the Rocky Ocean's shallower depths and is required to hook the Octopus.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select the Mighty Dildo Gift">
    The Octopus will ignore standard bait. Purchase and equip the **Mighty Dildo** from the shop inventory as your active attraction gift before launching your boat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Cast and Guide to 100 Meters">
    Cast your line into the Rocky Ocean. Carefully guide your lure down to the **100m** mark. Avoid bait-stealers on the way down by maneuvering your hook away from generic fish silhouettes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Fill the Hook Meter and Reel In">
    Once the Octopus engages, keep the tension steady to fill the hook meter. Once secured, reel the line back to the surface to complete the catch and claim your **1,500g** reward.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
If you attempt to dive without the correct gift equipped, the Octopus silhouette will swim near your lure but will never bite, wasting your valuable casting time.
</Callout>
