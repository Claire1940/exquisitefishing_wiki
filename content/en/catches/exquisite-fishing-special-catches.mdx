<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Farming & Progression Route">

To avoid getting stuck by expensive late-game boat upgrades, follow this optimized progression path. This route minimizes wasted gold and outlines exactly when to purchase new equipment.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Calm Ocean Capital">
    Begin in the Calm Ocean using the free Regular Old Boat. Purchase the **Diamond Ring** and **Regular Hook** to secure the Mermaid at 50m (300g). Once you have a small buffer, purchase the **Good Hook** and **Striped Pantie** to farm the Shark at 200m (2,000g). Finally, buy the **Box of Chocolates** and reuse your Regular Hook to land the Angler at 300m for a massive 10,000g payout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock the Rocky Ocean">
    Spend 1,500g to purchase the Regular Boat and unlock the Rocky Ocean. Prioritize buying the **Double Shielded Hook** and **Mighty Dildo** to catch the Octopus at 100m (1,500g). Progress deeper by purchasing the **Drill Hook** and **Pearl Necklace** for the Kappa at 500m (7,500g). Farm the 600m Angler (13,000g) using the **Reinforced Hook** and **Vibrant Rose** to build your capital.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conquer the Turbulent Ocean">
    Purchase the Reinforced Boat for 5,000g. Equip the **Super Hook** and **Diamond Ring** to target the high-value Shark at 900m, which rewards 24,000g per catch. After securing your funds, descend to the absolute bottom at 1,000m to retrieve **Grandpa's Letter**, a key collectible required to advance the narrative.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach the Dark Ocean Depths">
    Invest 8,000g in the Golden Boat to gain entry to the final biome. Upgrade your hooks in depth order, culminating in the **Omega Hook**. Pair it with the **Diamond Ring** to capture the Dark Ocean Angler at 2,000m. This is the deepest and most lucrative catch in the game, yielding 45,000g per successful attempt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Inventory Efficiency">
Many gifts are reused across different biomes. For instance, the Diamond Ring is used for the starting Mermaid, the mid-game Turbulent Shark, and the endgame Dark Ocean Angler. Do not sell off your older gifts, as buying them back will waste valuable gold.
</Callout>
