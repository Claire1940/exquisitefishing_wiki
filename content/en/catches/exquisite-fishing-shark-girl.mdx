<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Beginner Progression">

Mastering the core loop early ensures you do not waste gold on incorrect setups. Follow this progression pathway to optimize your early-game income.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Practice in the Calm Ocean">
    Begin in the Calm Ocean using the free Regular Old Boat. Focus on learning how to guide your lure close to targets without wasting casts on unmatched creatures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Secure Your First Special Catch">
    Equip the Regular Hook and purchase a Diamond Ring. Cast to 50m to land the Mermaid, which sells for a reliable 300g.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Target the Shark Girl">
    Upgrade to the Good Hook and purchase the Striped Pantie gift. Lower your line to 200m to catch the Shark Girl, yielding 2,000g.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Farm the Calm Ocean Angler">
    Equip the Regular Hook and a Box of Chocolates. Reach 300m to catch the Angler for a massive 10,000g payout, establishing your upgrade fund.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unlock the Rocky Ocean">
    Spend 1,500g to buy the Regular Boat. This grants you access to the Rocky Ocean and deeper, more valuable species.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Casting Safety">
Always ensure your equipped hook and gift match your target's requirements before casting. Lowering the wrong gear to depth will result in failed encounters and lost time.
</Callout>
