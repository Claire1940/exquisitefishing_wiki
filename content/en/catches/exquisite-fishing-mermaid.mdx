<Section icon={Target} title="Mermaid Unlock Requirements and Loadouts">

Unlocking every scene involving the **exquisite fishing mermaid** requires a deep understanding of the shop's inventory. These gifts are purchased from the shop and are consumed or checked upon interaction. As you progress into the Turbulent and Dark Oceans, the equipment requirements become significantly more expensive and specific.

The transition from the Calm Ocean to the Turbulent Ocean represents a major difficulty spike. The Reinforced Hook is necessary to handle the increased tension at 200m. Furthermore, the gift requirement shifts from the Diamond Ring to the Lovely Ribbon. If you attempt to catch the Mermaid with the wrong gift, she will simply ignore the lure, wasting your cast and time.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Step-by-Step Progression Walkthrough">

To efficiently unlock all content related to the **exquisite fishing mermaid**, players should follow a structured progression path. This prevents "gold-stalling," where a player spends all their money on a hook they cannot yet use in their current ocean.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Calm Ocean Grind">
    Start in the Calm Ocean with the Regular Old Boat. Your first goal is to purchase the **Diamond Ring**. Once equipped with the Regular Hook, dive to 50m and catch the Mermaid repeatedly until you have 1,500g.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrading the Vessel">
    Purchase the **Regular Boat** for 1,500g. This unlocks the Rocky Ocean. While the Mermaid does not have a unique scene here, the Rocky Ocean is vital for farming the **Reinforced Hook** and **Lovely Ribbon** needed for the next stage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Turbulent Waters">
    Invest 5,000g into the **Reinforced Boat** to enter the Turbulent Ocean. Equip the Reinforced Hook and Lovely Ribbon. Target the Mermaid at 200m to secure a 7,000g payout, which will fund your path to the Dark Ocean.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Deepest Depths">
    The final goal is the **Golden Boat** (8,000g). In the Dark Ocean, the Mermaid descends to 800m. You must have the **Super Hook** and **Pearl Necklace** to trigger this final encounter and complete her gallery entries.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Boat Tier | Access | Cost | Mermaid Opportunity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Regular Old** | Calm Ocean | 0g | 50m (300g) |
| **Regular Boat** | Rocky Ocean | 1,500g | N/A (Focus on Gold) |
| **Reinforced** | Turbulent Ocean | 5,000g | 200m (7,000g) |
| **Golden Boat** | Dark Ocean | 8,000g | 800m (20,000g) |
