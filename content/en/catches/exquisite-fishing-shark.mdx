To successfully land an **exquisite fishing shark**, you must match your loadout to the specific biome you are currently exploring. The game features three distinct Shark encounters, each tied to a specific depth and a unique "Gift" item that acts as bait. 

The following table breaks down the mandatory requirements for every Shark encounter documented in the 2026 version of the game.

| Biome | Depth | Required Hook | Required Gift | Sale Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Calm Ocean** | 200m | Good Hook | Striped Pantie | 2,000g |
| **Turbulent Ocean** | 900m | Super Hook | Diamond Ring | 24,000g |
| **Dark Ocean** | 500m | Drill Hook | Piece of Cake | 15,000g |

As shown above, the **Turbulent Ocean Shark** is the undisputed king of value, offering a massive 24,000g payout. However, reaching 900m requires the Reinforced Boat (5,000g) and the Super Hook, which is a late-game investment. Beginners should focus on the Calm Ocean Shark to build their initial bankroll.

<Callout type="warning" title="Equipment Check">
Standard bait does not work for special creatures. If you do not have the specific Gift (like the Striped Pantie or Diamond Ring) equipped in your secondary slot, the Shark will not spawn, regardless of your depth or hook type.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Shark Farming Route">

If your goal is to maximize gold efficiency using the **exquisite fishing shark**, follow this optimized progression route. This path minimizes wasted spending on intermediate gear that doesn't contribute to high-value catches.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Calm Ocean Kickstart">
    Begin by catching the Mermaid at 50m (300g) using the Regular Hook and Diamond Ring. Once you have enough, purchase the **Good Hook** and **Striped Pantie**. Target the Shark at 200m repeatedly until you have 1,500g.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Rocky Ocean Transition">
    Purchase the **Regular Boat** for 1,500g. While the Rocky Ocean doesn't have a Shark, use this area to catch the Angler at 600m (13,000g) to quickly save for the next boat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Turbulent Gold Mine">
    Spend 5,000g on the **Reinforced Boat**. Immediately equip the **Super Hook** and **Diamond Ring**. Dive to 900m to find the 24,000g Shark. This is the most efficient farming spot in the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dark Ocean Completion">
    After securing your wealth, buy the **Golden Boat** (8,000g). Equip the **Drill Hook** and **Piece of Cake** to catch the 500m Shark here for 15,000g, which is necessary for 100% gallery completion.
  </Step>
</Steps>
