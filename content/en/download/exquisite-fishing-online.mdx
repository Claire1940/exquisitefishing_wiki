<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Environmental Conditions & Timing">

Environmental factors play a massive role in the behavior and availability of fish. The time of day directly dictates how many fish spawn simultaneously in the river channels. During peak hours, players can observe up to four active ripples at once, whereas off-peak hours limit spawns to a single fish.

Dawn and dusk represent the absolute best times to cast your line. Nighttime is also highly productive, although the reflection of the moon on the water surface can make tracking ripples visually challenging. Daytime yields the lowest spawn rates, and thunderstorms will completely halt all biting activity.

  <Step num="2" title="Aim and Cast">
