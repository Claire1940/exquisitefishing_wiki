<Section icon={Swords} title="Gear Upgrades and Hook Combinations">

The heart of **exquisite fishing mobile** lies in its equipment system. To catch "Special Creatures," you must equip a specific combination of a **Hook** and a **Gift**. The shop sells various hooks that offer different levels of "Shielding" or "Drilling" capabilities, and gifts that act as bait for specific legendary targets.

### Essential Hook Progression
Hooks are not just about strength; they are about compatibility. A "Super Hook" might be great for late-game sharks, but it won't attract a basic Mermaid. You must constantly switch your loadout based on your current target.

| Hook Type | Progression Stage | Primary Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Regular Hook** | Starting | Catching Mermaids and basic Anglers |
| **Good Hook** | Early Game | Specifically for the 200m Calm Ocean Shark |
| **Double Shielded** | Mid Game | Required for Octopus and Whale targets |
| **Drill Hook** | Mid Game | Necessary for the Kappa and Dark Ocean Shark |
| **Omega Hook** | Endgame | The only hook capable of landing the 2,000m Angler |

### Strategic Gift Usage
Gifts are consumable items (though some mechanics allow for reuse) that attract the attention of the ocean's most elusive residents. Without the correct gift, a special creature will simply ignore your lure as it passes by.

| Gift Item | Target Creature | Required Pairing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Diamond Ring** | Mermaid / Shark | Pair with Regular or Super Hook |
| **Striped Pantie** | Shark / Kappa | Pair with Good or Anal Plug Hook |
| **Box of Chocolates** | Angler / Anemone | Pair with Regular or Reinforced Hook |
| **Mighty Dildo** | Octopus | Pair with Double Shielded or Anal Beads Hook |
| **Vibrant Rose** | Kappa / Angler | Pair with Drill or Reinforced Hook |

<Callout type="warning" title="Gear Compatibility">
Equipping a high-tier hook with a low-tier gift (or vice versa) often results in "No Interest" from special creatures. Always refer to your **Fish Report** or this guide to ensure your loadout matches the target's preferences.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Progression Walkthrough">

To reach the endgame of **exquisite fishing mobile** efficiently, follow this optimized spending and exploration route. This path minimizes wasted gold and ensures you always have the right gear for the next major milestone.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Calm Ocean Grind">
    Start by catching basic fish until you have enough for a **Diamond Ring**. Equip it with your **Regular Hook** and target the Mermaid at 50m. Use the 300g profit to buy a **Good Hook** and **Striped Pantie** to catch the 2,000g Shark.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Angler Payday">
    Before leaving the Calm Ocean, buy a **Box of Chocolates**. Use your **Regular Hook** to catch the Angler at 300m. This catch rewards a massive 10,000g, which is more than enough to buy the **Regular Boat** (1,500g) and enter the Rocky Ocean.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mid-Ocean Mastery">
    In the Rocky Ocean, prioritize the **Reinforced Hook**. This tool is versatile and allows you to catch the Anemone (300m) and the Rocky Angler (600m). Save up 5,000g for the **Reinforced Boat** to unlock the Turbulent Ocean.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Retrieving the Legacy">
    Once in the Turbulent Ocean, your main goal is twofold: farm the 900m Shark (24,000g) using the **Super Hook** and **Diamond Ring**, and dive to 1,000m to find **Grandpa's Letter**. This item is critical for the game's story progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Golden Abyss">
    Purchase the **Golden Boat** for 8,000g. This is the final vessel. You will now need to buy the most expensive hooks in the shop, culminating in the **Omega Hook**. Your final target is the Dark Ocean Angler at 2,000m, worth 45,000g.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
The 900m Shark in the Turbulent Ocean is widely considered the best "Gold per Minute" target in the game. If you find yourself short on cash for the final Golden Boat, farm this Shark 3-4 times to clear your debt.
</Callout>

</Section>
